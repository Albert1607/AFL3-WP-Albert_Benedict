Passions and Personal Development

Coding & Technology

I have a strong interest in coding and technology, particularly in software development and problem-solving. Through learning programming concepts such as Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), database design, and web development, I continuously challenge myself to build structured and efficient solutions.
Coding is not only a technical skill for me, but also a way to think logically and analytically. I enjoy transforming ideas into functional systems and exploring how technology can create real impact in business and daily life.

Photography

Photography is one of my creative outlets where I learn to observe details, lighting, and composition. Through capturing moments, I develop patience, perspective, and storytelling skills.
For me, photography is more than just taking pictures—it is about preserving emotions, documenting experiences, and communicating meaning visually. It also enhances my sense of aesthetics, which contributes to my creativity in digital and web design projects.

Music

Music plays an important role in shaping my focus and creativity. Whether listening or being involved in musical activities, I appreciate the discipline and emotional expression that music develops.
Music trains consistency, rhythm, and collaboration—qualities that are also valuable in academic and organizational environments.

Ministry / Volunteering

Being involved in ministry and volunteering activities allows me to grow in leadership, responsibility, and empathy.
Through serving others, I develop communication skills, teamwork, and the ability to manage events and people effectively. This experience shapes my character and strengthens my commitment to contributing positively to the community.
